How to play

  • Drag and drop to move a peg (indicated black circle).
  • Valid move is to jump over an adjacent peg into a hole (indicated empty square).
    • Jumped peg is removed.
  • The goal is to remove all peg except for a solitary peg -- I haven't implemented that judgment yet...
  • See wikipedia for more details on the rules.
